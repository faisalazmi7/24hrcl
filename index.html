ABOUT US

What We Do

We work with licensed Customized Living and Assisted Living providers to help them grow through marketing and pre–move-in coordination. To maintain quality and responsiveness, we partner with only a few providers at a time. Our role includes: ● Marketing licensed services ● Generating and qualifying referrals ● Supporting pre–move-in coordination after acceptance ● Communicating with case managers ● Preparing documentation for provider review We are not licensed and do not operate residential programs.

What We Do Not Do

We do not provide direct care

  • ● We do not admit residents
  • ● We do not supervise staff
  • ● We do not deliver services
  • ● We do not operate under the provider’s license

All care, staffing, and admissions are handled exclusively by the licensed provider.

HOW IT WORKS

Step 1: Program Review

We review your licensed services, capacity, and target audience to tailor marketing efforts.

Step 2: Marketing Setup

We develop compliant marketing materials and outreach strategies aligned with your services.

Step 3: Lead Generation

We market your home to generate interest from prospective residents and referral sources.

Step 4: Provider Review & Acceptance

The licensed provider reviews referrals and determines whether to accept the prospective resident.

Step 5: Pre–Move-In Coordination

Once accepted, our team coordinates pre–move-in requirements, including:

  • ● Completing the DHS 6790 with a qualified nurse
  • ● Communicating with case managers
  • ● Negotiating authorized service rates
  • ● Collecting approval documentation

Step 6: Provider Handoff

Once a move-in date is set, we send the provider:

  • ● Approved rate sheet
  • ● Completed and approved documents
  • ● Move-in confirmation

The provider completes admission and delivers all services under their license.
